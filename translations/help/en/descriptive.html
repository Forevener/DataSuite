Descriptive statistics

This page presents two types of analysis:

  • Nonparametric Descriptive Statistics
  • Parametric Descriptive Statistics

When you run any type of analysis on this page, any non-numeric variables and numerical variables with zero variance ​​are automatically removed from the data table.

All types of analysis on this page use the matrixStats package. They allow you to find out the general characteristics of the sample - the central trends, the range of the norm, the general deviance of values.

The choice of a parametric or non-parametric analysis method depends on the size and distribution of the sample.
